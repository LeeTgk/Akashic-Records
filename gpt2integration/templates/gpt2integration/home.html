O que é o GPT-2 Small Portuguese? (em inglês)

GPorTuguese-2 (Portuguese GPT-2 small) is a state-of-the-art language model for Portuguese based on the GPT-2 small model. It was trained on Portuguese Wikipedia using Transfer Learning and Fine-tuning techniques in just over a day, on one GPU NVIDIA V100 32GB and with a little more than 1GB of training data. It is a proof-of-concept that it is possible to get a state-of-the-art language model in any language with low ressources.
